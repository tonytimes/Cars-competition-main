How It Works

Score your dream car for just a fraction of the price!

Choose Your Dream Car

Explore our exciting competitions and select the car you’d love to win. Pick the number of tickets you want, answer a quick question, and hit 'Enter Now' to secure your chance!

Secure Your Tickets

Head to your basket, choose your payment method (PayPal, Debit Visa, Apple Pay, Google Pay), and lock in your ticket numbers. Your ticket details will always be available in your account for reference.

Guaranteed Winners

Each competition offers a fixed number of tickets, ensuring your odds are clear. Regardless of how many tickets are sold, the competition will proceed, giving you an even better chance to join our community of over 10,000+ winners.

Notification

Once the competition ends, we'll announce the winner on our website and notify them via email. Keep an eye on your inbox—you could be the next lucky car owner!
